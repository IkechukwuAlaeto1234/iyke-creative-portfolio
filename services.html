Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about my services and process

How long does a typical project take to complete?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A logo design typically takes 1-2 weeks, while a complete brand identity with website may take 4-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your payment structure?

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. For larger projects, I offer milestone-based payment plans to distribute costs throughout the project lifecycle.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, I provide 30 days of complimentary support after project completion to address any minor adjustments or questions. For ongoing needs, I offer maintenance packages for websites and regular design updates for marketing materials.

How many revision rounds are included?

The number of revision rounds depends on the package you choose. Basic packages include 1-2 rounds, while Professional and Premium packages include more extensive revision opportunities. Additional revisions beyond the included amount are available at an hourly rate.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide and have experience collaborating across different time zones. Communication is primarily conducted via email, video calls, and project management tools to ensure smooth collaboration regardless of location.
